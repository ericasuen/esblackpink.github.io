Jennie

Kim Jennie was Born and raised in South Korea, Jennie studied in New Zealand for five years before returning to South Korea in 2010. Then she decided to go audition for YG Entertainment.She was in the trainee program for nearly six years, and she is the longest of the members. Jennie also very fluent in English, Korean and Japanese. She is also a first member who debut solo music. Since she has a huge influence in fashion world, she become a brand ambassador for Chanel
