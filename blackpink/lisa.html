Lisa

Lisa (full name: Lalisa Manoban), commonly known as Lisa, is a Thai rapper, singer. She was born on March 27, 1997, in Bangkok, Thailand. Lisa joined YG Entertainment as a trainee in 2010 after successfully auditioning for the company in Thailand. She made her debut as a member of BLACKPINK in 2016. Lisa is known for her exceptional dance skills, stage presence, and charismatic rap verses. Lisa also speaks English and Japanese. In addition to her work with BLACKPINK, Lisa has pursued individual projects. She appeared as a mentor on the Chinese survival show "Youth With You".
